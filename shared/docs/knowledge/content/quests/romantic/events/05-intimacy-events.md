## RE-111: Первый поцелуй

**Категория:** Intimacy | **Relationship:** 70-80 | **DC:** 18 (Romantic)

### Триггеры
- Relationship 70+ (or 60+ if chemistry very high)
- Romantic moment (sunset, after date, special location)
- Privacy or semi-privacy

### Описание
Момент для первого поцелуя. Tension, anticipation, magic.

### Сценарии
1. **You initiate** (Romantic DC 18)
   - Успех: Perfect kiss, fireworks +30 relationship, "I've been waiting for this"
   - Провал: Awkward timing, NPC pulls back -5 relationship, "Not yet"
   - Крит.успех: Passionate kiss, can't stop +40 relationship, "I'm falling for you"

2. **NPC initiates** (Auto-success if chemistry 70+)
   - NPC kisses you → +35 relationship, Mutual desire

3. **Wait for perfect moment** (Romantic DC 14)
   - Build anticipation → +25 relationship when it happens

### Special первые поцелуи (региональные)
- **Under sakura** (Tokyo, Spring) → +10 bonus, Magical
- **Under Eiffel Tower** (Paris) → +15 bonus, Ultimate romance
- **In rain** (anywhere) → +12 bonus, Dramatic
- **At sunrise** (beach) → +10 bonus, New beginning
- **In snow** (Moscow winter) → +8 bonus, Warm in cold

### Следующие события
- → RE-112 (Ночь вместе - if ready), RE-113 (Откровение о прошлом)

## RE-112: Ночь вместе (First time)

**Категория:** Intimacy | **Relationship:** 75-85 | **DC:** 20 (Intimacy)

### Триггеры
- Relationship 75+
- After romantic evening
- At home (yours or NPC's)
- NPC ready (chemistry 80+)

### Описание
Первая интимная ночь вместе. Major step in relationship.

### Фазы
1. **Invitation** — "Want to come inside?" (Romantic DC 18)
   - Успех: NPC accepts +10 relationship
   - Провал: "Maybe next time" +2 relationship, Not ready yet

2. **Consent & Comfort** — Making sure both ready (Empathy DC 16)
   - Critical: Both must be comfortable
   - Успех: Mutual desire confirmed +15 relationship
   - Either uncomfortable: Stop, +10 relationship for respecting boundaries

3. **Intimacy** — Physical connection (Intimacy DC 20)
   - Успех: Beautiful intimate connection +40 relationship, "That was amazing"
   - Okay: Good but awkward first time +25 relationship, "We'll get better"
   - Провал: Too nervous +10 relationship, "Let's take it slow"

### Aftermath
- **Morning after** — Cuddling, breakfast
  - Stay and talk: +15 relationship, "I want more mornings like this"
  - Leave early: -10 relationship, "Was I just a hookup?"

### ВАЖНО: Cultural considerations
- **Conservative cultures:** May require marriage first (Middle East, Africa traditional)
- **Liberal cultures:** Casual approach (West Europe, USA)
- **Asian cultures:** Typically after commitment (relationship 80+)

### Следующие события
- → RE-113 (Откровение после), RE-120 (Morning after), RE-186 (I love you)

## RE-113: Откровение о прошлом

**Категория:** Intimacy | **Relationship:** 75-90 | **DC:** 22 (Empathy)

### Триггеры
- Relationship 75+
- After intimate moment
- High trust level

### Описание
После физической близости следует эмоциональная. NPC рассказывает самые глубокие секреты.

### Темы откровения
1. **Past trauma** — Abuse, violence, loss
   - Hold and comfort (Empathy DC 22) → +30 relationship, "You make me feel safe"
   - Can't handle → -20 relationship, "I can't share with you"

2. **Previous love** — Ex-partner story
   - Accept past (Empathy DC 20) → +25 relationship, "You're not jealous"
   - Jealous → -10 relationship, Insecurity

3. **Dark secret** — Crime, shame, regret
   - Not judge (WILL DC 24) → +35 relationship, "You accept all of me"
   - Judge → -40 relationship, Trust shattered

4. **Family secret** — Hidden family issues
   - Support (Social DC 20) → +28 relationship, "We'll face it together"

### Следующие события
- → RE-186 (Признание в любви), RE-114 (Showing vulnerability - mutual)

## RE-115: Встреча с семьёй NPC

**Категория:** Intimacy/Commitment | **Relationship:** 75-85 | **DC:** 24 (Social)

### КРИТИЧЕСКОЕ СОБЫТИЕ
Знакомство с семьёй NPC — может определить судьбу отношений.

### Подготовка
- Appropriate clothing (Cultural DC 16)
- Bring gift (Trading DC 14)
- Know cultural etiquette (Culture DC 18)

### Встреча с родителями
1. **Father** — Protective, tough (Social DC 24)
   - Успех: "You're good enough for my child" +40 relationship
   - Провал: "I don't approve" -30 relationship, **Relationship at critical risk**

2. **Mother** — Observant, judging (Empathy DC 22)
   - Успех: Mother loves you +35 relationship
   - Провал: "Not sure about this one" -20 relationship

3. **Siblings** — Protective or supportive (Social DC 18)
   - Успех: "Welcome to family!" +25 relationship

### Исход
- **Full approval:** +60 relationship, Clear path to marriage
- **Partial approval:** +20 relationship, Need to prove yourself
- **Disapproval:** -40 relationship, NPC may have to choose: family or you

### Cultural variations
- **Asian/Middle Eastern:** Family approval КРИТИЧНО (can make or break)
- **Western:** Important but not critical
- **African:** Family + community approval needed
- **Latin American:** Mother's opinion weighs heavily

## Механика интимности

### Intimacy Score (separate from Relationship)
- **Physical Intimacy:** 0-100 (sexual compatibility)
- **Emotional Intimacy:** 0-100 (trust, vulnerability)
- **Domestic Intimacy:** 0-100 (living together compatibility)

### For successful intimacy stage:
- Relationship 75+
- Emotional Intimacy 70+
- Physical Intimacy 60+
- Trust 80+

### Barriers to intimacy
- **Trust issues:** Past betrayal, -20 Emotional Intimacy
- **Physical insecurity:** Body issues, -15 Physical Intimacy
- **Commitment fear:** Past breakup, -20 Commitment readiness
- **Family disapproval:** -30 All intimacy if family against
