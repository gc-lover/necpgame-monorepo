## RE-166: Искренние извинения

**Категория:** Reconciliation | **После:** Any conflict | **DC:** 18 (Empathy)

### Триггеры
- Recent conflict (unresolved)
- Player initiates reconciliation
- NPC willing to listen

### Описание
Вы приходите к NPC с извинениями. Искренность критична.

### Выборы
1. **"I'm truly sorry"** (Empathy DC 18)
   - С eye contact, genuine emotion
   - Успех: "I forgive you" +20 relationship, Conflict resolved
   - Провал: "I need time" +5 relationship, Partial resolution

2. **Explain your side** (Persuasion DC 20)
   - Успех: "I understand now" +15 relationship, Mutual understanding
   - Провал: "That's an excuse" -5 relationship, Worse

3. **Actions speak louder** (Show don't tell)
   - Do something meaningful → +25 relationship, Proven sincerity

### Cultural variations
- **Japanese:** Formal apology, bow → +5 bonus
- **Latin:** Dramatic apology, passionate → +5 bonus
- **Russian:** Deep soul apology, philosophy → +5 bonus

## RE-167: Подарок-извинение

**Категория:** Reconciliation | **After:** Conflict | **DC:** 14 (Trading)

### Описание
Купить подарок как извинение. Материализм или искренность?

### Типы подарков
1. **Thoughtful gift** — Something NPC wanted (Trading DC 14)
   - Успех: "You remembered!" +18 relationship, Touched

2. **Expensive gift** — Jewelry, luxury (Cost 1000+ ed)
   - Успех: "You shouldn't have" +12 relationship, Appreciated
   - But: "Money can't fix everything" +5 relationship if serious conflict

3. **Handmade gift** — Personal, made by you (Crafting DC 16)
   - Успех: "This is so sweet" +25 relationship, Most touching

4. **Flowers** — Classic apology
   - +10 relationship, Safe choice
   - But: "I wanted to hear you say sorry" +5 if needs words

### Best approach: Gift + Words
- Gift alone: +15 relationship
- Words alone: +20 relationship
- Both: +30 relationship, Perfect apology

## RE-168: Грандиозный жест

**Категория:** Reconciliation | **After:** Major conflict | **DC:** 22 (Charisma)

### Триггеры
- Major conflict (severity 7+)
- Need big gesture to fix
- Public or spectacular action required

### Типы грандиозных жестов

1. **Public declaration** (Charisma DC 24)
   - Confess love publicly (radio, billboard, flash mob)
   - Успех: NPC swept off feet +40 relationship, "You're crazy!"
   - Провал: Too much, embarrassing -10 relationship

2. **Serenade** (Performance DC 22)
   - Hire mariachi/band, sing to NPC
   - Успех: Tears of joy +35 relationship, "So romantic"
   - Провал: Off-key but sweet +15 relationship, A for effort

3. **Epic quest** — Retrieve something meaningful
   - Complete impossible quest for NPC (Varies DC 26)
   - Успех: "You did this for me?" +45 relationship, Proven love

4. **Sacrifice** — Give up something important
   - Quit job, leave faction, etc. for NPC
   - → +50 relationship, "You chose me" (but may regret later)

### Regional grand gestures
- **Latin America:** Mariachi serenade traditional
- **Asia:** Public confession at landmark
- **Europe:** Flash mob at famous square
- **USA:** Billboard "I'm sorry" message
- **Russia:** Extreme gesture (freeze in cold, etc.)

3. **Therapy/Counseling** (Empathy DC 20)
   - Attending therapy → +30 relationship, "You're trying"

4. **Behavior modification** (WILL DC 20)
   - Anger management, etc. → +28 relationship, Growth

## RE-170: Couple's therapy

**Категория:** Reconciliation | **After:** Multiple conflicts | **DC:** 22 (Empathy)

### Триггеры
- Unresolved conflicts 3+
- Relationship at risk
- Both willing to try

### Session events
1. **Communication exercise** (Social DC 20)
   - Learn to listen → +15 relationship, "We never really talked"

2. **Root cause analysis** (Intelligence DC 20)
   - Discover underlying issues → +20 relationship, Understanding

3. **Commitment check** (Empathy DC 22)
   - Both still want this? → +25 relationship or Break up amicably

### Outcomes
- **Therapy success:** +40 relationship, Tools to resolve future conflicts
- **Partial success:** +20 relationship, Improved but not fixed
- **Failure:** +0 relationship, Maybe incompatible

### Cultural notes
- **Western:** Therapy accepted, encouraged
- **Asian:** Stigma against therapy (-5 if suggesting), prefer family mediation
- **Latin:** Prefer passionate reconciliation over clinical approach
- **Middle East:** Family mediation traditional

## Механика примирения

### Reconciliation Success Formula
```
Success_Chance = Base_DC - Apology_Quality - Time_Passed + Conflict_Severity

Apology_Quality:
  - Genuine emotion: -4 DC
  - With gift/action: -3 DC
  - Grand gesture: -5 DC
  - Half-assed: +5 DC

Time_Passed:
  - Immediate: +2 DC (too soon)
  - 1-3 days: +0 DC (good timing)
  - 1 week: +3 DC (too long)
  - 1 month: +6 DC (may be too late)

Conflict_Severity:
  - Minor (1-3): -2 DC
  - Moderate (4-6): +0 DC
  - Major (7-9): +4 DC
  - Critical (10): +8 DC
```

### Multiple conflicts
```
If Unresolved_Conflicts >= 3:
  Each additional conflict: +3 DC to reconciliation
  Relationship fatigue: -5 relationship per unresolved conflict
```

### Point of no return
- **Cheating confirmed:** 90% relationship ends
- **Violence against NPC:** 95% relationship ends
- **Betrayal to enemies:** 85% relationship ends
- **Repeated lies:** 70% relationship ends

## Cultural reconciliation styles

### Asian
- **Indirect apology:** Through actions, not words
- **Face-saving:** Private apology critical
- **Family mediation:** Elders help reconcile
- **Time:** Patient approach

### Western
- **Direct apology:** Words important "I'm sorry"
- **Therapy:** Professional help accepted
- **Quick resolution:** Address immediately
- **Independence:** "We need space"

### Latin
- **Passionate reconciliation:** Drama expected
- **Grand gesture:** Big romantic gesture required
- **Public apology:** Can be public, dramatic
- **Make-up passion:** Physical reconciliation important

### Russian/Slavic
- **Soul searching:** Deep philosophical talk
- **Vodka reconciliation:** Drink and talk
- **Patience:** May take time
- **Depth:** Shallow apology not enough

### Middle Eastern
- **Honor restoration:** Public honor важен
- **Family involvement:** Family may mediate
- **Formal apology:** Structured approach
- **Witnesses:** May need witnesses to reconciliation

### African
- **Community involvement:** Community may help
- **Ritual reconciliation:** Traditional ceremonies
- **Elder mediation:** Respect for elders
- **Public reconciliation:** Community witnesses
