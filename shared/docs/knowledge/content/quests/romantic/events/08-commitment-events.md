## RE-186: Признание в любви

**Категория:** Commitment | **Relationship:** 85-95 | **DC:** 20 (Romantic)

### Триггеры
- Relationship 85+
- Special moment (after intimacy, romantic location, etc.)
- Chemistry 80+

### Описание
Момент для "I love you". Первый раз говорим эти слова.

### Сценарии

#### Player initiates
1. **Direct confession** (Romantic DC 20)
   - "I love you"
   - Успех: "I love you too" +50 relationship, Mutual love confirmed
   - Провал: "I... I care about you" +10 relationship, Not ready yet
   - Крит.провал: Silence, -20 relationship, Too soon

2. **Romantic setting** (Romantic DC 18 with bonus)
   - At special place (sunset, under stars, etc.)
   - -2 DC from setting
   - Успех: "I've been waiting to hear that" +55 relationship

#### NPC initiates
- NPC: "I think I'm falling in love with you"
- Player response:
  1. **"I love you too"** → +60 relationship, Perfect mutual confession
  2. **"I care deeply"** → +20 relationship, Not quite there yet
  3. **"I need time"** → +5 relationship, NPC hurt but patient

### Aftermath
- First "I love you" unlocks deeper intimacy events
- Can say casually now ("Love you, bye!")
- Relationship enters committed phase

### Cultural variations
- **English:** "I love you" (straightforward)
- **French:** "Je t'aime" (passionate)
- **Spanish:** "Te amo" (serious) vs "Te quiero" (lighter)
- **Russian:** "Я люблю тебя" (very serious, not said lightly)
- **Japanese:** "愛してる" (aishiteru, VERY serious, rarely said) vs "好きだよ" (suki dayo, like you)
- **Arabic:** "أحبك" (serious, private usually)

## RE-187: Предложение переехать вместе

**Категория:** Commitment | **Relationship:** 90-100 | **DC:** 22 (Social)

### Триггеры
- Relationship 90+
- Dating for in-game months
- Spending lots of time together

### Описание
"Want to move in together?"

### Consideration factors
1. **Financial** (Trading DC 18)
   - Can afford place together?
   - Split costs fair?

2. **Lifestyle** (Social DC 20)
   - Compatible living habits?
   - Morning person vs night owl?

3. **Emotional readiness** (Empathy DC 22)
   - Ready for this step?
   - Fear of commitment?

### Outcomes
- **Yes:** +40 relationship, Living together (unlocks domestic events)
- **Not yet:** +10 relationship, "Let's wait a bit"
- **No:** -20 relationship, Different timelines

### Living together unlocks
- Shared apartment (Safe House)
- Domestic events daily
- Deeper intimacy
- Couple routines
- "Honey I'm home!" moments

## RE-188: Предложение руки и сердца

**Категория:** Commitment | **Relationship:** 95-100 | **DC:** 24 (Romantic)

### КРИТИЧЕСКОЕ СОБЫТИЕ
Marriage proposal. Ultimate commitment.

### Preparation
1. **Ring shopping** (Trading DC 20, Cost 5000-50000 ed)
2. **Plan proposal** (Planning DC 22)
3. **Family blessing** (Social DC 24) — Critical in some cultures
4. **Choose location** — Special place bonus

### Proposal moment
**"Will you marry me?"** (Romantic DC 24)

### Outcomes
- **Immediate Yes:** +100 relationship (max), Engaged!, Wedding planning starts
- **"I need to think":** +10 relationship, Uncertain (75% eventually yes)
- **"Not ready":** +5 relationship, Too soon (can try again later)
- **"No":** -30 relationship, Relationship may end or continue without marriage

### Cultural variations

#### Western (USA, Europe)
- **Public proposals:** Accepted, even expected
- **Expensive ring:** Important (2-3 months salary tradition)
- **Family blessing:** Nice but not required
- **Timeline:** 1-2 years dating typical

#### Asian
- **Family approval FIRST:** Must ask parents before proposing
- **Formal ceremony:** Proposal ceremony with families
- **Expensive:** Diamond ring + gifts for family
- **Timeline:** 2-3+ years, marriage-minded from start

#### Latin American
- **Grand gesture:** Public, dramatic preferred
- **Family central:** Must ask father's permission (traditional)
- **Ring:** Important but love more important
- **Timeline:** Can be quick (6 months) or long (3 years)

#### Middle Eastern
- **Family arranged:** May be family-initiated discussion
- **Dowry/Mahr:** Financial arrangements important
- **Religion:** Must be compatible religion (often)
- **Timeline:** Can be quick after family approval

#### African
- **Lobola/Bride price:** Traditional payment to family
- **Community:** Whole community involved
- **Ceremony:** Engagement ceremony with both families
- **Timeline:** After families agree

#### Russian/CIS
- **Serious:** Marriage very serious institution
- **Family approval:** Important
- **Traditional:** Formal proposal expected
- **Timeline:** After living together often

## RE-189: Встреча с родителями (proposal context)

**Категория:** Commitment | **Relationship:** 90+ | **DC:** 26 (Social)

### Триггеры
- Before proposal (traditional cultures)
- Or after acceptance (Western cultures)

### "Asking for hand in marriage"
- **Father:** "May I marry your daughter/son?" (Social DC 26)
  - Успех: "You have my blessing" +40 relationship (with NPC)
  - Провал: "I need to think" +0 relationship, Delayed
  - Failure: "No" -50 relationship, **Relationship crisis**, NPC must choose family or you

- **Mother:** Usually easier (Social DC 22)
  - Success: Mother's approval +35 relationship

### If family says no
- NPC choices:
  1. **Choose you:** Elope, -100 with family, +60 with you
  2. **Choose family:** Break up, -100 with you, "I'm sorry"
  3. **Try to convince family:** Extended quest chain
