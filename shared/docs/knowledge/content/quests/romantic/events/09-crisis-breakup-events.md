## RE-211: Подозрение в измене

**Категория:** Crisis | **Relationship:** Any 60+ | **DC:** 26 (Persuasion)

### Триггеры
- Misunderstanding
- Suspicious situation
- Found "evidence" (message, lipstick, etc.)

### NPC accusation
"I know you're cheating on me!"

### Defense options
1. **Truth (if innocent)** (Persuasion DC 24)
   - Успех: "I believe you" -15 relationship, Trust shaken but okay
   - Провал: "I don't believe you!" -40 relationship, **Relationship at critical risk**

2. **Explain situation** (Social DC 26)
   - Provide evidence of innocence
   - Успех: "I'm sorry I doubted" -10 relationship, Resolved
   - Провал: "Still suspicious" -30 relationship

3. **Get angry at accusation** (Charisma DC 28)
   - "How dare you accuse me!"
   - May backfire: -25 relationship, "Defensive means guilty"

### If actually cheated
- **Confess:** -80 relationship, 95% break up
- **Lie:** (Deception DC 30) — If caught later, -100 relationship, Definitely over

### Aftermath if resolved
- Trust damaged permanently (-10 max relationship)
- Jealousy events more common
- Need constant reassurance

## RE-212: Фракционный конфликт ultimate

**Категория:** Crisis | **Relationship:** 70-95 | **DC:** 28 (Social)

### Триггеры
- You and NPC on opposite sides of war
- Arasaka vs Militech final conflict
- Gang war involving both

### "Choose me or your faction"

### Choices
1. **Choose NPC** — Leave faction
   - +50 relationship, "You chose love over loyalty"
   - But: -100 faction, Hunted, Consequences

2. **Choose faction** — Leave NPC
   - +0 relationship, Break up, "I understand" (sad)
   - Keep faction loyalty

3. **Find third way** (Social DC 30)
   - Convince NPC to leave their faction
   - Or find neutral ground
   - Very difficult, may fail

### Tragic outcomes possible
- Romeo & Juliet scenario
- One dies protecting other
- Forced to betray one or the other
- No happy ending sometimes

## RE-213: Смертельная опасность

**Категория:** Crisis | **Relationship:** Any | **DC:** 28 (Combat/Medtech)

### Триггеры
- NPC in mortal danger
- Life-threatening situation
- Player must act fast

### Scenarios
1. **Combat save** (Combat DC 28)
   - Fight off attackers
   - Успех: NPC saved +50 relationship, "You saved my life"
   - Провал: NPC dies → Grief, Trauma, **Relationship ends permanently**

2. **Medical save** (Medtech DC 28)
   - NPC critically wounded
   - Успех: Stabilized +45 relationship, "I owe you my life"
   - Провал: NPC dies → Permanent loss

3. **Sacrifice play** — Take bullet for NPC
   - Player wounded/dies → +100 relationship (if survive), "Ultimate love"
   - If player dies: NPC grieves, may get revenge questline

### Aftermath
- If saved: Unbreakable bond, +50 relationship
- If failed: Grief events, depression, may never love again

## RE-214: Киберпсихоз partner

**Категория:** Crisis | **Relationship:** 70-95 | **DC:** 26 (WILL/Medtech)

### Триггеры
- NPC gets cyberpsychosis
- Too many implants, trauma trigger
- NPC dangerous

### "It's me, remember?"

### Options
1. **Talk down** (WILL DC 26, Empathy DC 24)
   - Успех: NPC recognizes you, calms down +30 relationship, "You saved me"
   - Провал: NPC attacks, forced to fight or flee

2. **Sedate** (Medtech DC 24)
   - Medical intervention
   - Успех: Stabilized +20 relationship, "Thank you for stopping me"

3. **Call MaxTac** — Professional help
   - NPC arrested/treated
   - +10 relationship, but -20 for calling authorities

4. **Let them go** — Can't handle it
   - → -50 relationship, NPC lost to cyberpsychosis, **Relationship ends**

### Recovery
- If saved: Long therapy process
- Reduce implants: NPC becomes "normal"
- PTSD from episode: New challenges
- Stronger bond: "You didn't give up on me" +40 relationship

## RE-215: Addiction crisis

**Категория:** Crisis | **Relationship:** 60-90 | **DC:** 24 (Empathy)

### Триггеры
- NPC's addiction (drugs, braindance, gambling) spirals
- Or player's addiction revealed

### "I need help"

### Response options
1. **Support recovery** (Empathy DC 22, Long-term commitment)
   - Help through withdrawal → +35 relationship, "You're my rock"
   - Attend meetings together → +25 relationship
   - Requires 30+ in-game days, multiple events

2. **Ultimatum** — "Get clean or we're done" (Social DC 24)
   - May work: NPC gets clean +30 relationship
   - May fail: NPC chooses addiction -100 relationship, Break up

3. **Enable** — Support addiction
   - → -40 relationship long-term, Co-dependent toxic relationship

4. **Leave** — Can't handle it
   - → Break up, +0 relationship, "I understand"

## RE-216: Pregnancy crisis

**Категория:** Crisis | **Relationship:** 80-95 | **DC:** 26 (Social)

### Триггеры
- Unexpected pregnancy
- Not planned
- May not be ready

### "I'm pregnant"

### Responses
1. **Joy** — "That's wonderful!" (if ready)
   - +50 relationship, "We're having a baby!"
   - Transition to family life

2. **Fear** — "I'm not ready" (honest)
   - Social DC 24 to discuss options
   - -20 relationship if not supportive

3. **Doubt** — "Is it mine?" (if suspicious)
   - Destroys trust: -80 relationship, May break up

### Options discussion
- Keep baby: +40 relationship, Commitment to parenthood
- Adoption: +20 relationship, Difficult choice together
- Termination: -30 relationship, Trauma for both (or +10 if mutual decision)
- Abandonment: -100 relationship, **Relationship ends**, You're deadbeat parent

## RE-231-250: BREAKUP EVENTS (20 событий)

### RE-231: "We need to talk"

**Категория:** Breakup | **Relationship:** Any | **DC:** Varies

The dreaded words. NPC or player initiates breakup.

### Reasons for breakup
1. **Grown apart** — "We're different people now"
   - Mutual, amicable: +10 friendship, Stay friends possible

2. **Cheating confirmed** — "I can't trust you"
   - -100 relationship, Hostile, May spread rumors

3. **Different life goals** — "We want different things"
   - Sad but mutual: +5 friendship, "I'll always care about you"

4. **Toxic relationship** — "This isn't healthy"
   - +20 for ending it, Better apart

5. **External pressure** — Family, faction, circumstance
   - "I have no choice" — Tragic, +0 relationship, Regret

---

### RE-232: Breakup aftermath stages

**Stage 1: Denial** (Day 1-3)
- "Maybe we can work it out?" (Persuasion DC 28)
- 20% chance to reconcile if wasn't too bad

**Stage 2: Anger** (Day 3-7)
- "How could you?!" — Angry texts
- -20 relationship if you respond angrily

**Stage 3: Bargaining** (Day 7-14)
- "What if we tried therapy?"
- 10% chance to reconcile

**Stage 4: Depression** (Day 14-30)
- Sad, alone, questioning
- Random encounters are awkward

**Stage 5: Acceptance** (Day 30+)
- "We're better as friends" or "Goodbye forever"
- +20 friendship if amicable, -50 if hostile

## Crisis mechanics

### Relationship Health Points
```
Relationship_HP = 100

Minor conflict: -10 HP
Moderate conflict: -25 HP
Major conflict: -40 HP
Crisis event: -60 HP

If HP <= 0: Relationship ends (breakup)
```

### Recovery
- Each reconciliation: +20 HP
- Time together: +5 HP per week
- Grand gesture: +30 HP
- Therapy: +25 HP

### Point of no return events
- Confirmed cheating: 95% break up
- Violence: 98% break up
- Betrayal to enemies: 90% break up
- Multiple crises unresolved: 85% break up

---

## Breakup types

### Mutual amicable
- Stay friends: +20 friendship
- No hard feelings
- May reconnect later (10% chance)

### One-sided
- Heartbreaker: -40 relationship, Guilt
- Heartbroken: -60 relationship, Grief

### Hostile
- Cheating, betrayal: -100 relationship, Enemies
- May sabotage your reputation
- Potential revenge questlines

### Tragic
- Death, circumstance: -100 relationship (gone)
- Grief questline
- May never love again (optional permanent effect)

---

## Post-breakup world

### NPC behavior after breakup
- **Amicable:** Polite in encounters, May help in quests still
- **Bitter:** Cold, avoids you, -10 reputation with their friends
- **Hostile:** Spreads rumors, May sabotage, -30 reputation
- **Grieving:** Sad, depressed, May leave city

### Player can:
- Try to get back together (Very hard, DC 30+)
- Move on to new romance
- Stay single
- Become commitment-phobic (-10 to all future romance)
